The Problem

Nigerian healthcare runs on memory and paper.

When a patient walks into a Lagos clinic, their doctor has no idea what medications they're on, what they're allergic to, or what the clinic down the street diagnosed them last week. Every visit starts from zero.

The EMR systems that exist were built for US hospitals with IT departments, stable internet, and dedicated trainers. They've failed Nigerian clinics for 20 years. We're not adapting them — we're replacing them.

What Alera Does

Built different.
From the ground up.

Not a digital filing cabinet. A clinical intelligence platform designed for how Nigerian healthcare actually works.

🎙
Voice-First Documentation
Doctor speaks naturally. AI fills the entire SOAP note, diagnosis, and prescription in seconds. No typing required.
✦
AI Diagnosis Assistant
Real-time differential diagnoses tuned for Nigerian conditions — malaria, typhoid, sickle cell, hypertension — with ICD-10 codes.

Patient Network (APID)
